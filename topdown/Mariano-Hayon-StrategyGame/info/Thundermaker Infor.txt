These are the abilities the Thundermaker does:

Energy Cell
-Enabled by default
-Costs 75 magic
-Freezes the enemy unit for a while

Energy Forcefield
-Costs 120 magic
-Protects units (yours and enemies) from long range attacks, not melee attacks (it's like Dark Swarm from the Starcraft Zerg Defiler)

Energy Storm
-Costs 100 magic
-Casts a single shock storm over the desired area, causing damage over all units
